# Trader Behavior Insights

## Objective
Analyze the relationship between trader performance and market sentiment (Fear vs Greed).

## Datasets
1. Hyperliquid Historical Trader Data
2. Bitcoin Fear & Greed Index

## Tools Used
Python
Pandas
Matplotlib
Seaborn

## Key Analysis
- Profit distribution during Fear vs Greed
- Trading volume comparison
- Buy vs Sell behavior
- Top profitable traders

## Key Insights
1. Traders showed higher profitability during Fear periods.
2. Trading volume increased significantly during Greed markets.
3. High trading frequency was associated with increased fees.

## Conclusion
Market sentiment plays a strong role in trader decision-making and profitability.
